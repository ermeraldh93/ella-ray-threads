Meet the Owners

Family Owned. Crafted With Care.

Hi! We're Monique & Robert.

We're the husband-and-wife team behind Ella Rae Threads.

What started as a passion for creating custom embroidered apparel has grown into a business dedicated to helping companies, teams, and individuals bring their ideas to life. Whether it's branded workwear, custom hats, team apparel, or one-of-a-kind boutique designs, every piece is embroidered with attention to detail, quality, and care.

As a family-owned small business, we believe every order deserves a personal touch. From the first conversation to the final stitch, we're committed to creating products you'll be proud to wear.

Thank you for supporting our small business—we truly appreciate every customer who trusts us with their vision. ✨
